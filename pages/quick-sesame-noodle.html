Quick Sesame Noodle

Base Ingredients:

  • 2-3 oz. Of your favorite noodle (I like using dried udon noodles)
  • 1 Tbs Soy sauce / tamari (or 1/2 Tbs if also using another high-salt ingredient like fish sauce)
  • 1 Tbs Toasted sesame oil

Optional Ingredients:

  • 1 Tbs Shaoxing wine
  • 1/2 Tbs Oyster sauce
  • 1/2 Tbs Fish Sauce
  • 1 Tsp Sugar
  • 1 Tbs Rice wine vinegar
  • 1 Tsp Chili crisp
  • 1 Tsp Toasted sesame seeds
  • 1 Tbs Minced Green Onion
  • 1 Tsp Instant Dashi (for flavoring the noodle cooking liquid)
  • 1 Tbs Crushed toasted peanuts

Steps:

  1. If using dashi, add to a pot of water and bring to a boil.
  2. While the water comes to a boil, prepare and combine all other ingredients in a bowl large enough to add the noodles as well.
  3. Add the noodles to the pot and cook until done to your liking.
  4. When the noodles are done cooking, add 1/4 cup of the cooking liquid to the mixing bowl, then drain the noodles and add to the bowl.
  5. Thoroughly toss the noodles with the other ingredients, serve and enjoy!

Additional Considerations:

  • If you prefer cold noodles on a hot summer day, run cold water over them after cooking and before combining with other ingreadients.
